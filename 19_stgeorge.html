St. George's Anglican Church


St. George's Anglican Church in Penang is a historic and revered religious institution located in George Town, the capital city of Penang, Malaysia. Established in 1818, it holds the distinction of being one of the oldest Anglican churches in Southeast Asia. The church is an architectural gem, characterized by its Georgian style and Palladian facade. Its serene presence and whitewashed exterior make it a prominent landmark in Penang's cityscape.


The church's location on Farquhar Street, within the UNESCO World Heritage Site of George Town, further adds to its significance. It stands amidst a tapestry of historical buildings, reflecting the multicultural heritage of Penang.


St. George's Anglican Church is not only cherished for its architectural splendor but also for its religious and cultural contributions. Regular services are held to cater to the spiritual needs of its congregation. The church also opens its doors to visitors, allowing them to experience its tranquil atmosphere and learn about its historical and architectural legacy.


Recognized as a national heritage site by the Malaysian government, the church has undergone preservation and restoration efforts to safeguard its structural integrity and cultural value. These endeavors ensure that the church remains a treasured symbol of Penang's rich history and diverse heritage.

Frequently Asked Questions


How do I get to St. George's Anglican Church?

You can get there by walking from Komtar (central bus terminal) in approximately 10-15 minutes, or by using a ride-sharing service or taxi to drop you off directly at the church.

Can I attend religious services at St. George's Anglican Church?

Yes, St. George's Anglican Church holds regular religious services, including Sunday worship services. You can check their schedule or contact the church office for specific service timings.

Can anyone visit St. George's Anglican Church?

Yes, St. George's Anglican Church is open to visitors. You can visit the church to admire its architecture, explore the grounds, or attend religious services.

Is photography allowed inside St. George's Anglican Church?

Photography is generally allowed inside St. George's Anglican Church, but it's always courteous to ask for permission if you wish to take photographs during a service or of any specific areas within the church.

Are there any entrance fees to visit St. George's Anglican Church?

No, there are no entrance fees to visit St. George's Anglican Church. However, donations are welcomed and appreciated to support the maintenance and preservation of the church.
