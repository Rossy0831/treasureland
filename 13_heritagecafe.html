Penang Heritage Cafes That You Must Go To Visit!

In recent times, the trend of enjoying coffee while engaging in conversations or business meetings has become popular, and Penang, known as the Pearl of the Orient, is not only renowned for its street art and delicious food but also for its diverse range of heritage cafes that offer exceptional coffee experiences. Have you had the chance to explore 8 of these charming establishments?


1. Patio


Patio, located in the bustling Penang Street, seamlessly combines vintage charm with modern elements, creating a serene environment perfect for relaxation; with its elegant setting, patrons can enjoy the tranquil atmosphere while choosing from six distinct layouts, including an urban courtyard, terrace lounge, and indoor dining options, making it an ideal destination to savor Spanish cuisine. Don't miss the opportunity to experience the enchanting ambiance of Patio today and take advantage of a 15% discount voucher valid for one month.



49F, Weld Quay, 10300 George Town, Malaysia.

012-3110442

Mon – Sun, 5.30pm till late (Kitchen Closed on Monday)



2. Cintra Heritage House


Located in the serene heritage town of Penang, the Cintra Heritage House offers a delightful retreat where visitors can immerse themselves in its cultural ambiance; with its traditional interior, fusion cuisine blending Western and local flavors, and convenient proximity to a boutique hotel, it's an inviting spot to enjoy delicious nibbles and experience the tranquility of this charming establishment. Don't miss the chance to indulge in the traditional charm of the Cintra Heritage House today!



Lot 1,2,5,7, Lebuh Cintra, 10100 Penang.

04-2628232

8.00am – 9.00pm (Daily)



3. Edelweiss Café Penang


Step into the historic Edelweiss Café, the first-ever shop house in Penang that boasts a remarkable age of 150 years and features fittings and fixtures dating back as far as 2,000 years, providing an authentic glimpse into Penang's rich heritage; serving as a unique "ancient café" and mini museum, visitors can delve into the history of Penang while enjoying delicious homemade delicacies, imported beers, and engaging conversations with the owner, who is also a knowledgeable tour guide, and don't forget to claim your complimentary dessert vouchers by spending a minimum of RM30 in a single bill. Immerse yourself in a nostalgic dining experience at Edelweiss Café and relive the charm of yesteryears.



38, Armenian Street, 10200, Georgetown, Penang.

04-261 8935

Tue – Fri: 11:00am – 3:00pm, 6:30pm -10:00pm, Sat: 12:00pm – 10:00pm, Sun: 12:00pm – 6:30 pm (Closed on Monday)



4. Jawi House Penang


Jawi House celebrates the rich Peranakan heritage of Penang by offering authentic Jawi Peranakan cuisine, prepared with traditional home recipes and complemented by exquisite homemade desserts; housed in a charming dwelling adorned with a captivating blend of Nyonya Peranakan and Chinese interior design, this culinary gem presents an unforgettable dining experience that showcases the aromatic spices and delectable flavors of Nyonya cuisine. Don't miss the opportunity to indulge in the exceptional offerings of Jawi House and immerse yourself in the cultural richness of Penang.



85, Lebuh Armenian, 10200 George Town, Penang.

04-2613680

Mon – Sat: 10.00am – 6:00pm, Private Dining: 7.00pm-11.00pm



5. Flash Back Penang


Flash Back provides a relaxed and welcoming haven for students seeking solace on weekends, doubling as a haven for bookworms and writers, while also offering a delightful dining experience with its distinctive and delectable dishes; renowned for its cozy and serene atmosphere, visitors frequent this charming establishment to unwind and indulge in their famous specialty, fried ice cream, with the added bonus of potential free French fry vouchers. Discover this remarkable destination and embark on a memorable experience soon.



No. 41 & 43, Lebuh Acheh 10300 George Town, Pulau Pinang.

016-6028696

12.00pm – 6.00pm (Daily)



6. China House Penang


China House provides a nostalgic journey as it comprises three heritage buildings in a traditional compound, complete with a charming open-air courtyard, boasting an astounding 14 distinct spaces within; nestled within Penang's UNESCO World Heritage site, this captivating haven attracts numerous visitors who seek to indulge in its renowned specialty: delectable baked goods. Upon arrival, you'll be greeted by an enticing array of cakes, adding an exquisite touch to your dining experience and evoking a delightful sensation. Experience the allure of China House and relish its delightful treats in a truly captivating setting.



153 & 155, Lebuh Pantai and 183B, Lebuh Victoria, 10300 George Town, Malaysia.

04-2637299

9.00am – 12.30am (Daily)



7. 55 Café


55 Café presents a one-of-a-kind haven that combines Mediterranean charm with a touch of modernity, featuring a rich heritage ambiance; in addition to serving delectable tapas, this establishment captivates coffee enthusiasts with its exceptional 3D art coffee, attracting a crowd eager to witness the remarkable coffee art and savor the high-quality coffee blends. During your visit, you may even have the chance to acquire F&B vouchers, adding to the appeal of this extraordinary destination. Embark on a memorable dining journey and experience the distinctive atmosphere of 55 Café.



55, Lorong Stewart, 10300 George Town, Penang.

04-2622611

8.30am – 10.30 pm (Daily)



8. Gala House


Gala House offers a captivating fusion experience that seamlessly blends culture and modernity within its walls, highlighted by a unique centerpiece—a Volkswagen car—creating an ideal backdrop for photography enthusiasts; centered around the concept of using spices, herbs, and no MSG, this extraordinary establishment invites patrons to indulge in delectable dishes such as the highly recommended Kimchi Salmon Spaghetti, Gala Steak, and Orang Kaya Coffee, all while enjoying exceptional service in a comfortable and inviting atmosphere.



102, Lebuh Muntri, 10200 George Town, Penang, Malaysia

04-2613118

12.00pm – 12.00am (Daily)
