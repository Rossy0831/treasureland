Penang Hill


Penang Hill, also known as Bukit Bendera, is a popular tourist attraction located in the Malaysian state of Penang. It is a hill resort situated in Air Itam, approximately 6 kilometers from the city center of George Town, the capital of Penang. Rising about 821 meters above sea level, Penang Hill offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area, including the cityscape, Strait of Malacca, and the mainland. It is a place of natural beauty and tranquility, attracting both locals and tourists alike.


One of the main attractions on Penang Hill is the Penang Hill Railway, a funicular railway that takes visitors up to the summit. The railway, which has been operating since 1923, offers a scenic ride through lush greenery and provides an easy and convenient way to reach the top.


At the summit of Penang Hill, visitors can explore various attractions and recreational facilities. The Owl Museum showcases a collection of owl-related artifacts and artwork, while the Monkey Cup Garden features a diverse range of pitcher plants. Additionally, there are several restaurants and food stalls serving local delicacies, making it a great place to experience Penang's renowned cuisine.


Nature enthusiasts can take leisurely walks along the nature trails, enjoying the cool climate and observing the local flora and fauna. There are also well-maintained gardens, such as the David Brown's Strawberry Hill, where visitors can relax and enjoy the beautiful surroundings.


Penang Hill is a popular spot for locals to escape the heat and enjoy cooler temperatures, especially during the hot summer months. It provides a peaceful retreat away from the bustling city and offers a unique vantage point to appreciate the beauty of Penang.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is the operating hours of Penang Hill?

Penang Hillâ€™s ticketing counters are open every day, 6.15am to 8.00pm while the funicular service runs from 6.30am to 10.00pm (last trip from the Upper Station to the Lower Station).

Please take note that the funicular service will be shut down for its bi-annual scheduled.

What is the Penang Hill Railway?

The Penang Hill Railway is a funicular railway system that transports visitors from the Lower Station to the summit of Penang Hill. It has been operating since 1923 and offers a scenic ride through the hill's lush greenery.

Are there any other ways to reach the summit of Penang Hill?

Yes, besides the Penang Hill Railway, you can also hike up the hill using various nature trails available. The trails vary in difficulty and offer a more adventurous and nature-oriented experience.

What are the attractions at Penang Hill?

Penang Hill offers a range of attractions and activities. Some popular ones include the Owl Museum, Monkey Cup Garden, Canopy Walk, and Love Lock. There are also several viewing decks, gardens, and restaurants to explore.

Are there any restaurants or food options available?

Yes, there are several restaurants and food stalls at Penang Hill that serve a variety of local delicacies and snacks. You can enjoy traditional Malaysian cuisine, refreshments, and even afternoon tea at some of the dining establishments.

What is the climate like on Penang Hill?

Penang Hill is known for its cooler climate compared to the lowlands. The temperature is generally a few degrees lower, providing a pleasant escape from the heat. It is advisable to bring a light jacket or sweater, especially in the evenings.

Is there an entrance fee to visit Penang Hill?

Yes, there is an entrance fee for Penang Hill, which includes the Penang Hill Railway ride. The fee varies for adults, children, and senior citizens. Additional charges may apply for certain attractions or activities.
