3 Best Areas To Stay In Penang

Planning your first trip to Malaysia and unsure of where to stay in Penang? The task can be challenging due to the wide range of accommodations available, varying in quality and style. From budget-friendly backpacker hostels to luxurious five-star resorts that rank among the best in Penang, the choices can be overwhelming.


To alleviate the stress of searching for the perfect place to stay, this comprehensive guide provides you with all the information you need. It covers the top areas, neighborhoods, and hotels in Penang, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience for travelers like yourself.




1. GEORGE TOWN


Best area for first-timers, foodies & culture lovers


When you’re on a quest to decide where to stay in Penang, there’s no better choice than George Town. The island’s urban centre is the most compelling destination in Penang.


The UNESCO World Heritage area in the heart of George Town is the vibrant center of Penang, where you'll find numerous attractions and activities. This eclectic district showcases a blend of British colonial architecture, traditional Chinese merchant houses, and significant temples and mosques. With a mix of shopping options, local eateries, captivating street art, and renowned street food, George Town emerges as the perfect choice for your stay in Penang, offering an immersive and unforgettable experience.

2. BATU FERRINGHI


Best area for nightlife & adventures by the sea


When the bustling atmosphere of George Town becomes overwhelming, escape to the serene coastal allure of Batu Ferringhi. While there may be superior beaches in other parts of Malaysia, the white sandy expanse in and around this seaside town offers some of the most exceptional waterfront views in Penang.


The town exudes a relaxed and beachfront ambiance, making it an ideal destination for water-loving travelers. Enjoy swimming or engage in thrilling activities like parasailing, waterskiing, paddle-boarding, or windsurfing.


Batu Ferringhi is also famous for hosting Penang's most popular night market, a lively event offering a wide range of products at discounted prices. From kitschy souvenirs to imitation designer fashion accessories, you can find it all here. The vicinity of the night market is also home to a collection of bars, providing a perfect setting to unwind and enjoy the sunset.

3. Bayan Lepas


Best area for late-night arrivals & early-morning departures


Bayan Lepas is a practical choice for travelers arriving late or departing early from Penang International Airport. Additionally, the area offers unique attractions such as The War Museum, the Snake Temple, and Penang Aquarium. Shoppers will also appreciate the newly built Queensbay Mall, the largest of its kind in Penang, making Bayan Lepas a great destination for retail therapy.
